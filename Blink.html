Blink!

This was our first Arduino workshop, where we built a simple circuit to make an LED blink using digital output and a breadboard. It introduced us to basic components, wiring, and how code controls hardware through pin connections. It helped me understand and visualise the flow and direction of current (5v Pin > Resistor > Switch > LED > Ground). We were also taught about the role of the resistor which reduces the voltage preventing damage to the LEDs.
